About

MockBuster is THE premier DVD/VHS collection mock Point of Sale system this side of Bend, Oregon. Utilizing several new (to me) technologies, including SQL and state of the art API's, along with Flask for Python. Created in 2021 as a summer project it has grown to be utilized by nearly a quarter of a dozen people. This project is offered as is and there is no guarantee of its precision. Thank you for visiting.
